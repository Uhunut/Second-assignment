About Jack Black

Born: August 28, 1969

Jack Black is an American actor, comedian, and musician known for his energetic performances and comedic genius. He rose to fame with his roles in movies like "School of Rock" and "Kung Fu Panda." Black is also the lead vocalist of the comedy rock duo Tenacious D. His unique style and contributions to entertainment have made him a beloved figure worldwide.
